Ortho & True Ortho Photo Services

Ortho and True Ortho Photos are advanced imaging solutions that provide accurate and detailed representations of landscapes, infrastructure, and built environments. These services play a crucial role in various industries, offering unparalleled precision in mapping, analysis, and visualization.

Our Services:

Ortho Photos:

Our Ortho Photo services involve the production of high-resolution, georeferenced aerial images with minimal distortion. These images are meticulously corrected for terrain relief, camera tilt, and lens distortion, ensuring an accurate representation of the Earth's surface.

True Ortho Photos

True Ortho Photos take precision to the next level by combining high-resolution imagery with digital surface models. This results in orthorectified images that eliminate building lean and provide a true-to-scale representation of features on the ground. True Ortho Photos are ideal for applications requiring the utmost accuracy.

Applications:

Urban Planning: Accurate mapping for infrastructure development and city planning. Engineering: Detailed topographic maps for engineering projects and land management. Agriculture: Precision agriculture, crop monitoring, and land-use planning. Environmental Monitoring: Assessing land surface changes, habitat quality, and natural resource management.
